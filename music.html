Personal

Ever since getting my first iPad Mini, I have been fascinated with music production. Putting together GarageBand loops and messing with different audio effects have always been one of the major outlets of my creativity.

I mostly listen to and produce electronic music spanning a variety of sub-genres as a hobby. Additionally, I listen to hip-hop and classical.
